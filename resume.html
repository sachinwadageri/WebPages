How to Write a Great Resume

  • Keep it short (1 page for freshers, max 2 pages for experienced).
  • Use strong action words: "Led", "Developed", "Achieved".
  • Tailor your resume for each job application.
  • Highlight measurable achievements (e.g., “Improved efficiency by 20%”).
